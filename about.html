Our Story

Welcome to Tito Macho's Filipino Catering Service, your number one source for Filipino Food and catering service. Founded in 2022, by Prince, Tito Macho's Filipino Catering Service has come a long way from its beginnings in his own home. When Prince first started out, his passion for Filipino food drove him to start his own business. What started as a hobby, became a passion as he shared his love of cooking and culture with his friends and family.

What We Do

Here at Tito Macho's Filipino Catering Service, we are dedicated to providing you with the very best of filipino food and catering service, with an emphasis on great food, exceptional customer service and reasonable prices.
